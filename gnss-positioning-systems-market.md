<p><p>The GNSS positioning systems market is broadly categorized into core satellite navigation systems and regional satellite navigation systems. Core satellite navigation systems, such as GPS, GLONASS, Galileo, and BeiDou, provide global coverage for accurate positioning and timing information. In contrast, regional satellite navigation systems focus on smaller geographic areas, enhancing performance through improved accuracy and reduced signal latency. These systems serve specialized applications in aviation, surveying, and disaster management, complementing global networks to enhance overall navigation capabilities.</p></p>
