<p><p>The dental implant system market is categorized into three main types: implants, abutments, and crowns. Implants are artificial tooth roots surgically placed in the jawbone to support prosthetic teeth. Abutments connect the implant to the crown and provide stability. Crowns are the visible part of the artificial tooth, designed to restore function and aesthetics. Together, these components form a comprehensive solution for tooth replacement, enhancing oral health and improving the quality of life for patients with missing teeth.</p></p>
