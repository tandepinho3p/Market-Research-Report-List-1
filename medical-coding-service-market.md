<p><p>The medical coding service market encompasses various classification systems, primarily the International Classification of Diseases (ICD) and the Healthcare Common Procedure Coding System (HCPCS). ICD provides codes for diagnosing conditions, enabling standardized health data exchange. HCPCS, on the other hand, focuses on billing for procedures, services, and supplies in the healthcare system. Together, these coding systems enhance administrative efficiency, improve accuracy in billing, and facilitate better healthcare management by ensuring comprehensive data capture and reporting.</p></p>
