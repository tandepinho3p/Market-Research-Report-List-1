<p><p>The drug-eluting bioabsorbable stent market is primarily categorized into sirolimus and paclitaxel drug-eluting stents. Sirolimus drug-eluting stents release sirolimus, an immunosuppressant that inhibits cell proliferation, reducing the risk of restenosis. Paclitaxel drug-eluting stents, on the other hand, utilize paclitaxel, a chemotherapeutic agent that prevents cell growth. Both stent types aim to enhance vascular healing while minimizing long-term complications, making them vital in treating coronary artery disease and improving patient outcomes through effective drug delivery and gradual absorption.</p></p>
