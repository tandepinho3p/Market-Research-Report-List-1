<p><p>The Peripheral Artery Angioplasty market encompasses two primary types: balloon angioplasty and stent implantation. Balloon angioplasty involves inserting a catheter with a deflated balloon into narrowed arteries, which is then inflated to widen the vessel, improving blood flow. Stent implantation is often performed alongside balloon angioplasty, where a small mesh tube is placed in the artery to maintain its openness. Together, these techniques address peripheral artery disease, enhancing patient outcomes and quality of life by restoring vascular health.</p></p>
