Disinfectant spray systems deploy chemical solutions to sanitize individuals and surfaces upon entry, effectively targeting harmful microorganisms. Heat disinfection employs high temperatures to eradicate bacteria and viruses, often suitable for materials that can withstand heat. Collectively, these methods enhance hygiene and safety in public spaces.</p></p>
