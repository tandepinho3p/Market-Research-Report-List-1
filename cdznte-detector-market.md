<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The CdZnTe Detector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pixelated</li><li>Coplanar Grid</li><li>Planar-Type</li><li>Quasi-Hemispherical</li><li>Housed Quasi-Hemispherical</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The CdZnTe detector market comprises several types, each serving different applications. Pixelated detectors feature an array of small, discrete pixels for high spatial resolution, ideal for imaging. Coplanar grid detectors use a two-dimensional electrode configuration for efficient charge collection and are suited for spectroscopy. Planar-type detectors offer a simple design for general purposes. Quasi-hemispherical detectors provide improved energy resolution and sensitivity for specific applications. Housed quasi-hemispherical detectors integrate the benefits of the latter with protective housing for enhanced durability and performance.</p></p>
