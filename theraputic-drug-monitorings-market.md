<p><ul><li>Chemiluminescence Immunoassay (CLIA)</li><li>Fluorescence Immunoassay (FPIA)</li><li>Radioimmunoassay (RIA)</li><li>Colorimetric Immunoassay</li><li>Other Immunoassays</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The therapeutic drug monitoring market encompasses various assay types to measure drug levels in patients. Chemiluminescence immunoassays (CLIA) utilize light emission for detection, offering high sensitivity. Fluorescence immunoassays (FPIA) use fluorescent tags for quantification, providing rapid results. Radioimmunoassays (RIA) employ radioactive isotopes for measurement, known for their precision but limited by safety concerns. Colorimetric immunoassays rely on color change reactions, making them simple and cost-effective. Other immunoassays include diverse techniques tailored for specific therapeutic drugs, enhancing individualized patient care.</p></p>
