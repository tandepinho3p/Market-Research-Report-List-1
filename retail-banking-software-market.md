<p><p>Retail banking software can be categorized into two main types: cloud-based and on-premises solutions. Cloud-based software is hosted on remote servers, offering scalability, flexibility, and reduced IT costs, making it suitable for banks seeking to innovate rapidly. In contrast, on-premises solutions are installed locally on the bank's own servers, providing greater control over data security and compliance, but often requiring higher upfront investments and ongoing maintenance. Each type caters to different operational needs and strategic goals for retail banks.</p></p>
