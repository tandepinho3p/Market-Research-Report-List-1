<p><p>The Drop Weight Tear Tester (DWTT) is an apparatus used to evaluate the toughness of materials, particularly in assessing the fracture behavior of steel. The market is divided into two main types: low energy and high energy. Low energy DWTT focuses on simulating ductile-to-brittle transitions in materials under lower impact energy, suitable for standard applications. In contrast, high energy DWTT targets extreme conditions, examining materials’ resistance to fracture in high-stress environments, critical for industries like oil and gas.</p></p>
