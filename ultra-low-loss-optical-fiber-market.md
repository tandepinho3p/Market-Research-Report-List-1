<p><strong>The Ultra Low-loss Optical Fiber Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Multimode Fiber</li><li>Single-mode Fiber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Ultra Low-loss Optical Fiber market is primarily segmented into two types: multimode fiber and single-mode fiber. Multimode fiber, with a larger core diameter, is designed for short-distance communication, supporting multiple light modes simultaneously, making it ideal for data centers and internal networks. In contrast, single-mode fiber has a smaller core, allowing only one light mode to propagate, which enables long-distance transmission with minimal signal loss. Both types cater to specific applications depending on transmission distance and performance requirements.</p></p>
