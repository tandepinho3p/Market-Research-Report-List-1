Single-channel meters are designed for standalone measurements, ideal for simple applications. Dual-channel meters allow simultaneous measurement of two samples, enhancing efficiency in comparative analysis. Multi-channel meters can measure multiple samples at once, making them suitable for high-throughput laboratories. Each type offers unique advantages, catering to different laboratory needs and workflow requirements.</p></p>
