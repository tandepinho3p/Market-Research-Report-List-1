<p><p>The Behind-the-Ear (BTE) hearing aids market encompasses two primary types: Digital Signal Processing (DSP) hearing aids and Simulated Signal Processing hearing aids. DSP hearing aids utilize advanced algorithms to analyze and process sound digitally, providing better clarity and noise reduction. In contrast, Simulated Signal Processing hearing aids offer a more basic level of sound processing, mimicking natural hearing by amplifying sounds without the sophisticated analytics of DSP. Together, they cater to varying degrees of hearing loss and user preferences.</p></p>
