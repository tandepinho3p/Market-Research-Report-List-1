<p><p>The compressor thermal overload protectors market features two primary types: external thermal overload protectors and built-in thermal overload protectors. External thermal overload protectors are standalone devices installed outside the compressor to monitor temperature and prevent overheating. In contrast, built-in thermal overload protectors are integrated directly into the compressor assembly, providing streamlined protection without needing separate installation. Both types are essential for enhancing compressor reliability and efficiency, preventing damage, and ensuring safe operation in various applications, including HVAC and refrigeration systems.</p></p>
