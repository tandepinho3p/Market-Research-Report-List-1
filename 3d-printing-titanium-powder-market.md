Ti64ELI (Extra Low Interstitial) offers improved ductility and fracture toughness, catering to specialized medical uses such as implants. The "Others" category encompasses other titanium alloys and grades customized for specific industrial applications, expanding the material's versatility in additive manufacturing.</p></p>
