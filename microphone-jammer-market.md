<p><p>The Microphone Jammer market is segmented into two primary types: Ultrasonic Interference and Speech-like Interference. Ultrasonic Interference utilizes high-frequency sound waves beyond human hearing to disrupt microphone functionality, effectively blocking audio capture. On the other hand, Speech-like Interference generates noise that mimics human speech patterns, creating confusion and masking actual conversations. Both methods aim to protect privacy by thwarting unauthorized audio recording, catering to various sectors such as personal security, corporate environments, and sensitive communications.</p></p>
