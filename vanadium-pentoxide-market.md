The Dry Method involves calcining vanadium ore in a furnace, producing a highly pure and dense product, favored for industrial applications. Conversely, the Wet Method utilizes leaching processes with acids to extract vanadium, resulting in a solution that is then processed to create vanadium pentoxide.
