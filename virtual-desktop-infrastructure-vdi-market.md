The VDI market is categorized into two main types: On-Premise VDI, where organizations manage and host virtual desktops within their own data centers, offering greater control and security; and Cloud-Based VDI, where desktops are hosted on third-party cloud platforms, providing scalability, flexibility, and reduced infrastructure costs. Both models enable remote work and efficient resource utilization.</p></p>
