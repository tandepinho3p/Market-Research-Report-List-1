<p><strong>The Foam Bottle Technology Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Extrusion Moulding Technology</li><li>Blow Moulding Technology</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Foam Bottle Technology encompasses various manufacturing processes for producing lightweight, insulated bottles. Extrusion moulding technology involves shaping molten foam material into a continuous form, which is then cut into bottles, ensuring consistent wall thickness. Blow moulding technology, on the other hand, forms bottles by inflating a heated foam tube into a mould, allowing for complex shapes and designs. Other methods may include injection moulding or rotational moulding, each offering unique advantages in terms of production efficiency, cost, and design flexibility.</p></p>
