The KP type is known for its high expansion ratio and is often used in applications requiring superior thermal insulation and fire resistance. On the other hand, the Low S type features a lower expansion ratio, making it suitable for applications where less expansion is necessary. Both types serve critical roles in various industries, including construction, automotive, and electronics, providing tailored solutions based on their specific properties.</p></p>
