<p><ul><li>Styrenic-Butadiene-Styrenic (SBS)</li><li>Hydrogenated Styrenic Block Copolymers (HSBC)</li><li>Styrene-Isoprene-Styrene (SIS)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The SBC market comprises various types of block copolymers, each serving unique applications. Styrenic-Butadiene-Styrenic (SBS) is known for its excellent elasticity and is widely used in adhesives and sealants. Hydrogenated Styrenic Block Copolymers (HSBC) offer enhanced thermal stability and UV resistance, making them suitable for roofing and industrial applications. Styrene-Isoprene-Styrene (SIS) provides good processability and flexibility, often utilized in modified bitumen and medical devices. Together, these materials cater to diverse industries, including automotive, construction, and consumer goods.</p></p>
