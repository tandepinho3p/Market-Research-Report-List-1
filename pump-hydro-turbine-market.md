<p><p>The pumped hydro turbine market comprises two main types: single-stage and multi-stage systems. Single-stage systems use one set of turbines to pump water and generate electricity, making them simpler and typically more cost-effective for smaller applications. In contrast, multi-stage systems utilize multiple turbine stages, enhancing efficiency and allowing for greater elevation changes. This makes them suitable for larger-scale energy storage and generation, offering flexibility in meeting varying energy demands and improving grid reliability.</p></p>
