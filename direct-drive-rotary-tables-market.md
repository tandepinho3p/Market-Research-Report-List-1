<p><p>The Direct Drive Rotary Tables market consists of two primary types: External Rotation Direct Drive Turntables and Internal Rotation Direct Drive Turntables. External rotation tables execute motion around an axis positioned outside the table’s surface, allowing for versatile applications in machining and assembly. Conversely, internal rotation turntables have their rotation mechanics located within the table, providing a compact design ideal for precision tasks and limited spaces. Both types enhance performance, eliminate gear backlash, and improve accuracy in various industrial applications.</p></p>
