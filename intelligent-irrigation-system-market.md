<p><p>The Intelligent Irrigation System market is primarily categorized into weather-based and sensor-based controllers. Weather-based controllers utilize real-time meteorological data to optimize watering schedules, ensuring efficient water usage based on current and forecasted weather conditions. In contrast, sensor-based controllers incorporate soil moisture, temperature, and other environmental sensors to monitor the landscape's actual water needs, delivering irrigation precisely when and where needed. Both types aim to enhance water conservation, improve crop health, and reduce operational costs in agricultural and landscaping applications.</p></p>
