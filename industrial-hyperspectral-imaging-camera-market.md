<p><strong>The Industrial Hyperspectral Imaging Camera Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pushbroom Hyperspectral Cameras</li><li>Snapshot Hyperspectral Cameras</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Industrial Hyperspectral Imaging Camera market is primarily segmented into two types: Pushbroom Hyperspectral Cameras and Snapshot Hyperspectral Cameras. Pushbroom cameras capture images line by line as the camera moves, making them ideal for applications requiring high spatial resolution and extensive area coverage. In contrast, Snapshot Hyperspectral Cameras capture an entire scene in one exposure, enabling rapid imaging with minimal motion artifacts, which is beneficial for dynamic or high-speed environments. Both types are essential for diverse industrial applications, including agriculture, food quality inspection, and materials analysis.</p></p>
