<p><ul><li>Single Lumen Balloon Catheters (SLB)</li><li>Multi Lumen Balloon Catheters (MLB)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The brachytherapy catheter market includes single lumen balloon catheters (SLB) and multi lumen balloon catheters (MLB). SLB catheters consist of a single channel that delivers radioactive sources directly to the treatment area, ensuring precise targeting of tumors. In contrast, MLB catheters have multiple lumens, allowing for simultaneous delivery of radiation to several sites or enhanced distribution of treatment. Both types are essential in radiation therapy for cancer, offering tailored approaches to patient care and improving treatment outcomes.</p></p>
