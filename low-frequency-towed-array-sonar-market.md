<p><p>The Low Frequency Towed Array Sonar market is divided into two main types: passive sensors and active sensors. Passive sensors detect sounds from submarines and other underwater vehicles by listening for acoustic signals in the ocean, making them stealthy and effective for surveillance. In contrast, active sensors emit sound waves and analyze their echoes to locate underwater objects, providing precise range data. Both types are crucial for naval operations, enhancing situational awareness in underwater combat scenarios.</p></p>
