<p><ul><li>Stackable Dehydrators</li><li>Shelf Dehydrators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The food dehydrators market can be categorized into stackable and shelf dehydrators. Stackable dehydrators consist of multiple trays that can be stacked vertically, allowing for efficient use of space and flexibility in capacity. They are often compact and suitable for small batches. Shelf dehydrators, on the other hand, feature a fixed shelf design with larger trays, providing consistent airflow and easier access to food. Both types cater to varying consumer needs, from casual users to more serious food preservers.</p></p>
